ABOUT THE COURSES

We provide a three-year course in Creative Learning. Creativity is a vast aspect and we promise to cover every side of it. Arts, Fashion and Multi-media are our centres of focus. Multiple opportunities provided along with scholarships.

What You Will Learn

Creative thinking enhances your indefinite interest. It helps to embrace your originality. Creative genre builds up a unique personality and helps you to grow psychologically. Your self-esteem will boost up.

We include a vast range of creative offers fit for every student seeking a creative institution. Fashion Designing, Art, Interior Decoration, Film-Making, and various computer graphic and designing courses, photo, video editing, etc.

  • Art including drawing classes, sketches, 3D techniques
  • Fashion, style, makeup designing
  • Computer animation, game design, 3D computer modeling.

We provide equal space for every student to create their own innovations. Placement opportunities also available depending on the students’ performance. Expert guidance and practical classes are provided for better understanding. All courses are career-oriented and time efficient.
